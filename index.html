Club Awards

Where the Standard is Set

These awards recognize sales associates and team leaders for exceptional annual performance.

Award Details

Individual sales associates and team members earn an Individual Club Award, while Team Club Awards are presented to team leaders based on their team’s total GCI for the calendar year. Individual and team member plates showcase the affiliate’s name and award year, while team leader plates proudly display the team’s name and year earned.

Agents that have earned a Club Award are honored live during the “Best of REMAX” awards celebration at R4®. Registered R4 attendees can pick up their trophies during the event or at the trophy booth in the Marketplace.

Award designations are then updated in each affiliate’s MAXCenter profile by the second week of February each year.

For international affiliates, commissions used to determine award levels are converted to U.S. dollars using an approved REMAX conversion rate, and only commissions paid within, or shortly after the award year, count toward qualification.

Career Awards

Where Excellence Becomes Legacy

Career Awards are earned through an associate’s cumulative individual GCI over their time with REMAX.

Award Details

Beginning with Lifetime Achievement, these honors also include a length of affiliation requirement, and both criteria, earnings and tenure, must be met to qualify.

The Circle of Legends, Luminary of Distinction and Paramount of Excellence awards are presented live during the Opening General Session at R4, while Hall of Fame and Lifetime Achievement honorees are celebrated during the high-energy “Best of REMAX” awards celebration.

If Hall of Fame or Lifetime Achievement winners can’t attend R4, they can still proudly display their accomplishment by purchasing their trophy through remax.crownawards.com.

Circle of Legends, Luminary of Distinction and Paramount of Excellence trophies are shipped automatically upon award confirmation.

Award status updates appear in each affiliate’s MAXCenter profile within 90 days of confirmation. For affiliates outside the U.S. and Canada, commissions are converted to U.S. dollars using an approved REMAX rate, and only commissions paid within (or shortly after) the required tenure count toward qualification.

Top 25/50/100

Individual and Team Rankings

The Top 25/50/100 Rankings are determined by total gross paid commissions earned within the year, showcasing those who consistently operate at the highest level.

Ranking Details

To qualify for the team rankings, groups must achieve a minimum of $40,000 USD in year-to-date commissions, setting a strong standard of performance. Team size is determined monthly by counting all team members plus the team leader as of the last day of that month, ensuring every ranking reflects the true structure and strength of each team.

Individual Rankings include the Top 100 (Worldwide/U.S./Canada/Global) Residential and Commercial.

Team Rankings include small teams (two people), medium teams (three-to-nine-person team), large teams (10+ person team) and Top 100 (Worldwide/U.S./Canada/Global) Residential and Commercial combined.

Award Details

The Special Awards are presented each year at R4 and honor the network’s highest achievers, our elite producers, powerhouse offices and most respected leaders.

At the heart of these recognitions is the Distinguished Service Award. This honor is given to individuals whose leadership, character and contributions have made a lasting, meaningful impact on the network — embodying the very best of REMAX.

For production and growth awards, winners are selected across the U.S., Canada and Global regions, with the highest performing affiliate earning the honor of worldwide winner. Commission and transaction-based awards are thoughtfully separated into Individual and Team categories to ensure fair and accurate recognition. Only commissions paid within the current award year (or shortly thereafter) are counted toward qualification.

Many office-level categories include further distinctions, honoring excellence across metro and small markets, as well as recognizing both single franchise and multi-franchise operations.

Award Definitions
  • Single vs. Multi Franchise:

    Single Franchise - A franchise with a single franchise agreement (a single office with a team office that has only one franchise agreement would be considered a Single Franchise)
    Multi-Franchise - A franchise with two or more franchise agreements

  • Net Growth Index:

    The difference between a prior period agent count and the current agent count with adjustments to exclude agent activity attributed either to transfers from other REMAX offices during the evaluation period or to re-activations within 30 days after termination of REMAX affiliation.

  • Small vs Metro Market:

    Small Market - 30,000 or less in populations that are substantially distant from major urban area
    Metro Market - 30,000 or more in population

  • Special Nomination Awards:

    Distinguished Service, Broker/Owner of the Year Single Franchise, Broker/Owner of the Year Multi Franchise and Region of the Year are determined through a nomination process. Regions submit nominations in December and the final selections are made at REMAX World Headquarters in January. Recipients are notified that they’ve won a Special Award but are not given specific details.

State Level Awards

State-level awards spotlight the top performers within each state, celebrating excellence in the markets where agents and broker/owners make the greatest local impact.

Through a combination of performance-based honors, culture and community impact awards and special recognitions for leadership and service, we highlight the individuals and offices that demonstrate excellence throughout the year.

  • Brand Ambassador

    The Brand Ambassador Award recognizes individuals who consistently embody the REMAX brand values, actively participate in company events, and go above and beyond in representing the network. Recipients demonstrate exceptional commitment to REMAX and their clients, serving as true stewards of the brand and setting the standard for excellence.

  • Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals

    Top Agent, Top Brokerage and Top Team: Awarded to the Agent/Brokerage/Team with the highest contributions to CMN.

  • Eagle Award

    Awarded to the brokerage with the highest Network Growth Index (NGI) for the year (NGI is the net agent change after the removal of transfers & reactivations within 30 days of leaving).

  • Highest Average Transactions Per Associate

    Awarded to the brokerage with the highest average transactions per associate

  • Manager of the Year

    Awarded to a Manager of the Office who embodies the values of REMAX. This person inspires all-around excellence as they consistently exceed their duties. Trainings, engagement opportunities and other learning events beyond those offered through LLC, are just a few examples of how this person positions their office to thrive. The Manager of the Year is a resource to the office, an outstanding team member and an invaluable component to the success of the brokerage.

  • Outstanding Brokerage of the Year

    The Outstanding Brokerage of the Year Award honors an exceptional brokerage in the state for the year. They’ve cultivated a successful and dynamic atmosphere in their brokerage, and fully committed to enhancing the careers and lives of their sales associates, team members and clients. They embrace a REMAX culture of achievement, collaboration, professionalism and an “everybody wins” approach to business. They also exhibit tremendous pride in being part of the REMAX network.

  • Recruiter of the Year

    Awarded to the brokerage with the highest combination of New Adds & Reactivations, awarding the office for recruiting the most agents to REMAX.

  • Top Producer

    This report is published monthly with MTD, and then YTD, at the end of each quarter.

  • Rookie of the Year

    Awarded to an agent licensed during the award year with the highest GCI. Rookie can be with a team or an individual. Team designation does not impact the award.

  • Total Volume Achievement Award

    Awarded to all brokerages with a total brokerage volume of $100+ million.

  • Top Three Closed Transactions (Individual & Team)

    Awarded to the top three individual and top three teams based on closed transactions.

  • Top Commercial Commissions (Individual & Team)

    Awarded to the top individuals and top teams based on commercial GCI.

  • Top Residential and Commercial Commissions (Individual & Team)

    Awarded to the top individuals and top teams based on combined residential and commercial GCI.

  • Top Producer Monthly Reports

    Each month a top producer report (individual and team RES/COM) is posted to MAXCenter and a quarterly YTD report is posted at the end of each quarter.

Marketing Resources

  • Show off your trophies and certificates proudly. Let them shine where clients can’t miss them.
